When playing Rong, the left paddle (the player in Versus AI and Zen mode, and one of the players in multiplayer) may be moved up and down using the 'w' and the 's' keys respectively. If free movement is enabled, it may be moved left and right using the 'a' and 'd' keys respectively, and rotated counter clockwise and clockwise using the 'q' and 'e' keys respectively.

Similarly, if you are playing multiplayer, the right paddle may be moved up and down using the 'i' and 'k' keys respectively. And if free movement is enabled, it may be moved left and right using the 'j' and 'l' keys respectively, and rotated counter clock wise and clock wise using the 'u' and 'o' keys respectively.
